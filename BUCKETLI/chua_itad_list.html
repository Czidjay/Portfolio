Ultimate Bucket List

Jenilyn Chua

MYSELF FAMILY / FRIENDS

With Myself

This list is all about personal growth, experiences I want to have, and milestones I hope to reach. Some are major life goals, while others are just things I’ve always wanted to try for the fun of it. Either way, these are the things that would make me feel fulfilled and excited about life.

Graduate with Flying Colors

  • I’ve always wanted to make the most out of my education, not just for the sake of finishing, but to really excel. There’s something about knowing I gave it my all that would make graduation even more rewarding.

Find a Job I Love

  • Work takes up a huge part of life, so I want to do something I enjoy. A job that excites me, challenges me, and keeps me motivated every day would be ideal.

A Job with a Healthy Work Environment

No matter how great a job is, if the workplace is toxic, it’s not worth it. I want to be in an environment where people respect each other, where I feel valued, and where I can grow.

Become Debt-Free

Having financial freedom would mean less stress and more opportunities to enjoy life without constantly worrying about money.

Adopt a Pet Dog

I like cute dogs, and giving one a home would be something really special. Having a furry friend to care for and bond with would be amazing.

Try a Crazy Hair Color

It’s just one of those things that seems fun to do at least once in life. Maybe something bold like blue or purple.

Learn to Do Pottery or Ceramics

Creating something from scratch and getting my hands dirty in the process sounds fun and therapeutic. Plus, I’d love to make my own handmade pieces.

Touch or Hold a Monkey, Koala, Dolphin, and Elephant

I love animals, and the idea of getting up close with these creatures is exciting. It would be such a cool experience.

Achieve Financial Stability and Independence

Being able to support myself, save for the future, and still enjoy life comfortably is something I aim for.

Achieve a Specific Career Milestone

Whether it’s a promotion, leading a big project, or reaching a position I’ve always wanted, I want to accomplish something meaningful in my career.

Get Married or Start a Family

If and when the time feels right, I’d love to build a life with someone special and maybe even start a family.

Build and Live in My Dream Home

I’ve always imagined what my dream home would look like, and one day, I hope to make it a reality, a place that truly feels like mine.

With My Family & Friends

Some of the best experiences in life are meant to be shared. This list is all about the trips, adventures, and unforgettable moments I want to experience with the people closest to me. Whether it’s traveling to new places or doing something exciting together, these are the memories I want to create with my family and friends.

Travel Around the World

There’s so much to see and experience, and I want to explore as many places as I can with the people I care about.

Go to Every Disneyland

Disneyland is pure fun and nostalgia, and I want to visit all of them to experience each park’s unique magic.

Go to Every Universal Studios

As a fan of movies and theme parks, visiting every Universal Studios would be an epic adventure.

Go Snorkeling or Scuba Diving

Exploring the underwater world and seeing marine life up close would be an unforgettable experience.

Go Paragliding or Sky Diving

The thrill of flying through the air, feeling completely free, and seeing the world from above, it’s a rush I want to experience.

Go on a Hot Air Balloon Ride at Sunrise or Sunset

Floating above beautiful landscapes while the sky changes colors. It’s the kind of experience that would leave a lasting memory.

Go Horseback Riding

Riding a horse through scenic trails or along a beach sounds peaceful and fun. Maybe when we go back on a holiday to Baguio

See a Show on Broadway

I love live performances, and Broadway is the ultimate theater experience.

Go on a Cruise Holiday

A vacation on a ship, waking up to different destinations, and just relaxing on the ocean sounds perfect.

Go Bungee Jumping

I’m not sure if I’d actually go through with it, but the idea of taking that leap and feeling the rush is thrilling.

See the Northern Lights

This is one of the most beautiful natural sights in the world, and I want to witness it in person at least once.

Go Gun Shooting

Just to try it out at a legit shooting range, test my aim, and experience something different.
