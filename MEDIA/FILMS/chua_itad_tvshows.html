My Film TV Shows

ANIME K-DRAMA CRIME/MYSTERY

Anime

Detective Conan: Case Closed (1996)

Shinichi Kudo, a brilliant teenage detective, is transformed into a child after being poisoned by a secret organization. Under the alias Conan Edogawa, he solves complex cases while trying to uncover the truth behind the organization and regain his original body.

Creator: Gosho Aoyama (Manga)

Main Voice Cast (Japanese): Minami Takayama (Conan Edogawa), Kappei Yamaguchi (Shinichi Kudo), Wakana Yamazaki (Ran Mouri), Rikiya Koyama (Kogoro Mouri)

Assassination Classroom (2015)

A mysterious octopus-like creature, Koro-sensei, becomes the homeroom teacher of Class 3-E, a group of misfit students. He challenges them to assassinate him before he destroys Earth while also guiding them to become better individuals.

Director: Seiji Kishi

Main Voice Cast (Japanese): Jun Fukuyama (Koro-sensei), Mai Fuchigami (Nagisa Shiota), Tomokazu Sugita (Karma Akabane), Shizuka Itou (Irina Jelavić)

Naruto (2002)

Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest Hokage, embarks on a journey of growth, friendship, and battles against powerful enemies, all while carrying the burden of a dangerous nine-tailed fox sealed within him.

Creator: Masashi Kishimoto (Manga), Hayato Date (Anime)

Main Voice Cast (Japanese): Junko Takeuchi (Naruto Uzumaki), Noriaki Sugiyama (Sasuke Uchiha), Chie Nakamura (Sakura Haruno), Kazuhiko Inoue (Kakashi Hatake)

K-Drama

Taxi Driver (2021)

A mysterious taxi service called "Rainbow Taxi" operates as a secret vigilante group, avenging victims who have been failed by the justice system. Kim Do-ki, an ex-special forces officer, leads the team in taking down criminals.

Director: Park Joon-woo

Main Cast: Lee Je-hoon (Kim Do-ki), Esom (Kang Ha-na), Kim Eui-sung (Jang Sung-chul), Pyo Ye-jin (Ahn Go-eun)

A Business Proposal (2022)

Shin Ha-ri, a young woman struggling financially, goes on a blind date pretending to be her friend—only to discover that her date is Kang Tae-moo, the CEO of her company. When he unexpectedly proposes a fake relationship, things take a comedic turn.

Director: Park Seon-ho

Main Cast: Ahn Hyo-seop (Kang Tae-moo), Kim Se-jeong (Shin Ha-ri), Seol In-ah (Jin Young-seo), Kim Min-kyu (Cha Sung-hoon)

Mouse (2021)

A rookie detective, Jung Ba-reum, gets caught in a deadly game with a serial killer. As he investigates, shocking secrets about psychopathy and genetic predisposition to violence unfold, blurring the lines between good and evil.

Directors: Choi Joon-bae, Kang Cheol-woo

Main Cast: Lee Seung-gi (Jung Ba-reum), Lee Hee-joon (Go Moo-chi), Park Ju-hyun (Oh Bong-yi), Kyung Soo-jin (Choi Hong-joo)

Crime / Mystery

NCIS (2003)

The Naval Criminal Investigative Service (NCIS) team investigates crimes involving the U.S. Navy and Marine Corps. Led by Special Agent Leroy Jethro Gibbs, the team solves cases ranging from espionage to terrorism.

Creator: Donald P. Bellisario, Don McGill

Main Cast: Mark Harmon (Leroy Jethro Gibbs), Michael Weatherly (Tony DiNozzo), Pauley Perrette (Abby Sciuto), Sean Murray (Timothy McGee), David McCallum (Dr. Donald "Ducky" Mallard)

High Potential (2024)

Morgan, a single mother with an extraordinary knack for solving complex crimes, finds herself assisting the police despite having no formal training. Her unique perspective makes her an invaluable asset in investigations.

Creator: Drew Goddard

Main Cast: Kaitlin Olson (Morgan), Daniel Sunjata (Karadec), Javicia Leslie (Daphne)

Criminal Minds (2005)

The FBI’s Behavioral Analysis Unit (BAU) profiles and tracks down serial killers and dangerous criminals by analyzing their psychological patterns. The team deals with high-stakes cases while balancing their personal struggles.

Creator: Jeff Davis

Main Cast: Matthew Gray Gubler (Dr. Spencer Reid), Thomas Gibson (Aaron Hotchner), Kirsten Vangsness (Penelope Garcia), Shemar Moore (Derek Morgan), Joe Mantegna (David Rossi)
