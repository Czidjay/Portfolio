My Film Movies

ANIMATION FANTASY HORROR

Animation

Coraline (2009)

A young girl named Coraline Jones discovers a secret door in her new home that leads to an alternate world where everything seems perfect. However, she soon realizes that the Other World hides a dark secret, and she must find a way to escape before it's too late.

Director: Henry Selick

Main Cast: Kiana Madeira (Deena), Olivia Scott Welch (Samantha Fraser), Benjamin Flores Jr. (Josh), Sadie Sink (Ziggy Berman), Gillian Jacobs (C. Berman)

Shrek (2001)

A grumpy ogre named Shrek embarks on a quest to rescue Princess Fiona in exchange for regaining his swamp from annoying fairytale creatures. Along the way, he befriends a talkative donkey and discovers that Fiona has a secret of her own.

Directors: Andrew Adamson, Vicky Jenson

Main Cast: Mike Myers (Shrek), Eddie Murphy (Donkey), Cameron Diaz (Princess Fiona), John Lithgow (Lord Farquaad)

Tangled (2010)

Rapunzel, a princess with long magical hair, has been locked away in a tower her entire life. When a charming thief, Flynn Rider, stumbles upon her tower, she embarks on an adventurous journey to discover the world beyond her small home.

Directors: Nathan Greno, Byron Howard

Main Cast: Mandy Moore (Rapunzel), Zachary Levi (Flynn Rider), Donna Murphy (Mother Gothel)

Fantasy

Into the Woods (2014)

A musical fantasy that intertwines several classic fairy tales, including Cinderella, Little Red Riding Hood, Jack and the Beanstalk, and Rapunzel, as they all seek to fulfill their wishes. However, their happily-ever-afters come with unexpected consequences.

Director: Rob Marshall

Main Cast: Meryl Streep (The Witch), Emily Blunt (The Bakerâ€™s Wife), James Corden (The Baker), Anna Kendrick (Cinderella), Chris Pine (Prince Charming)

Alice in Wonderland (2010)

Alice, now a teenager, falls back into the fantastical world of Wonderland, where she must embrace her destiny to defeat the Red Queen and restore peace with the help of familiar characters like the Mad Hatter and Cheshire Cat.

Directors: Tim Burton

Main Cast: Mia Wasikowska (Alice), Johnny Depp (Mad Hatter), Helena Bonham Carter (Red Queen), Anne Hathaway (White Queen)

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (2001)

An orphaned boy, Harry Potter, discovers he is a wizard on his 11th birthday and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. There, he learns about his past, makes friends, and faces the dark wizard who killed his parents.

Director: Chris Columbus

Main Cast: Daniel Radcliffe (Harry Potter), Emma Watson (Hermione Granger), Rupert Grint (Ron Weasley), Richard Harris (Albus Dumbledore), Alan Rickman (Severus Snape)

Horror

The Conjuring (2013)

Paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren are called to help a family experiencing terrifying supernatural events in their new home. They uncover a malevolent entity that threatens to destroy the family.

Director: James Wan

Main Cast: Patrick Wilson (Ed Warren), Vera Farmiga (Lorraine Warren), Lili Taylor (Carolyn Perron), Ron Livingston (Roger Perron)

Insidious (2010)

A couple moves into a new house only to experience disturbing supernatural occurrences. When their son falls into an unexplainable coma, they discover he has the ability to astral project and is trapped in a dangerous dimension called "The Further."

Director: James Wan

Main Cast: Patrick Wilson (Josh Lambert), Rose Byrne (Renai Lambert), Ty Simpkins (Dalton Lambert), Lin Shaye (Elise Rainier)

Fear Street Trilogy (2021)

A group of teenagers in the town of Shadyside uncover the centuries-old curse that has plagued their town with brutal killings. They must fight to break the cycle of horror before it's too late. The trilogy spans three different time periods: 1994, 1978, and 1666, each revealing more about the curse.

Director: Leigh Janiak

Main Cast: Kiana Madeira (Deena), Olivia Scott Welch (Samantha Fraser), Benjamin Flores Jr. (Josh), Sadie Sink (Ziggy Berman), Gillian Jacobs (C. Berman)
